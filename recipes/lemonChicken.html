Lemon Herb Chicken

This lemon-herb chicken is a simple, quick, and delicious dish. All you need are a few herbs, a lemon, and of course, the chicken! The amount of spices is completely up to you. You can add more or less according to your taste.

    Ingredients

  • 2 (5 oz) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pinch driend oregano
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

      • Directions

  • Place chicken in a bowl; pour 1/2 of the lemon juice over chicken and season with salt.
  • Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium-low heat.
    Place chicken into hot oil.
    Add remaining lemon juice and oregano; season with black pepper.
    Cook chicken until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5 to 10 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  • Garnish chicken with parsley to serve.
